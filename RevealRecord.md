# RevealRecord
---
- **Name:** RevealRecord
- **Description:** Reveals a piece of evidence from the court record.
- **Parameters:**
  - **Evidence name:**  
    The name of the evidence you wish to reveal.

- Examples:
```
1:  RevealRecord:["Autopsy Report"];
```
