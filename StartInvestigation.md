# StartInvestigation
---
- **Name:** StartInvestigation
- **Description:** Starts a new investigation and waits until every investigation is finished.
- **Parameters:**
  - **Investigation ID:**  
    The ID of the first investigation to play.

- Examples:
```
1:  StartInvestigation:[0];
```

- Remarks:
> None
