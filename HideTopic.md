# HideTopic
---
- **Name:** HideTopic
- **Description:** Hides a talking topic in an investigation.
- **Parameters**
  - **Investigation ID:**  
    The ID of the investigation containing the talking topic.
  - **Index of the topic:**  
    The 1-based index of the topic.

- Examples:
```
1:  HideTopic:[0|1];
```

- Remarks:
> None
