# HideMove
---
- **Name:** HideMove
- **Description:** Hides a move option in an investigation.
- **Parameters:**
  - **Investigation ID:**  
    The ID of the investigation containing the move option.
  - **Index of the move option:**  
    The 1-based index of the move option.

- Examples:
```
1: HideMove:[0|1];
```

- Remarks:
> None
