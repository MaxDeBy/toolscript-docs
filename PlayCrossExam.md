# PlayCrossExam
---
- **Name:** PlayCrossExam (CE)
- **Description:** Starts a cross examination process.
- **Parameters:**
  - **Cross examination ID:**  
    The ID of the cross examination you want to play.

- Examples:
```
1:  PlayCrossExam:["BadBoyIsLying"];
2:  CE:[4];
```

> None
