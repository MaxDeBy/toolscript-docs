# HideRecord
---
- **Name:** HideRecord
- **Description:** Hides a piece of evidence from the court record.
- **Parameters:**
  - **Evidence name:**  
    The name of the evidence you wish to hide.

- Examples:
```
1:  HideRecord:["Autopsy Report"];
```
