# StopMusic
---
- **Name:** StopMusic (SM)
- **Description:** This instruction stops a track of music.
- **Parameters:**
  - **Delay:**  
    The amount of miliseconds the engine should wait before stopping the music. For example to have the music be cut by a sound effect.

- Examples:
```
1:  StopMusic:[600];
2:  SM:[0];
```

- Remarks:
> None
