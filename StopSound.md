# StopSound
---
- **Name:** StopSound
- **Description:** Stops a sound effect or ends a sound loop.
- **Parameters:**
  - **Sound name:**  
    The name of the sound effect to be stopped.

- Examples:
```
1: StopSound["SFX"];
```

- Remarks:
> None
