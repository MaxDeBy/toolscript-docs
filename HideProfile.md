# HideProfile
---
- **Name:** HideProfile
- **Description:** Hides a profile from the court record.
- **Parameters:**
  - **Profile name:**  
    The name of the profile you wish to hide.

- Examples:
```
1:  HideProfile:["Mia Fey"];
```
