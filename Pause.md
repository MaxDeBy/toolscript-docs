# Pause
---
- Name: Pause
- Description: Tells the engine to wait a certain amount of time before executing the next line in the script.
- Parameters:
  - **Delay**:  
    The amount of miliseconds the engine should wait before continuing.

- Examples:
```
1:  Pause:[1000];
```

- Remarks:
>None
